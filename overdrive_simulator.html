2. Race Calendar

The calendar is revealed before cars are auctioned — study the circuits to decide which car to bid on. Shuffle randomly or pick manually.

3. Car Auction

Each player starts with 12 Credits. Cars are auctioned one at a time in a random order. Bidding is open — any player may bid. Highest bidder claims the car and pays from their starting 12 Cr. Opening bid: 1 Cr. You cannot bid more Credits than you have remaining after the auction (you still need Credits to buy upgrades in Race 1).

4. Start Championship

Complete the car auction before starting.
